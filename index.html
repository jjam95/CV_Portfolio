Welcome to my Portfolio

My name is Julie Jane Mason and I am an amalgamation of everyone I meet, flaws and all, with a dash of individuality - stitched together with good intentions. As a Clinical Psychology Graduate, tech was not the original destination I had in mind. However, through a combination of desire for change, tenacity to begin again and a desire for new passions, it truly has come to be the path I needed to throughly engage and challenge me.

I am currently working towards my AWS Cloud Practitioner certification as part of the AWS re/Start programme, with a keen aim towards entering employment in cloud computing. As a newbie to tech with a psychology background, I look forward to bringing a fresh perspective to the field and I am excited for the opportunities that it will inevitably lead me to. Less than 10% of IT is in the cloud you say? Well that just means there is over 90% of innovation just waiting at my fingertips.
